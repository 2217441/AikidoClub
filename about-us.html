About Us

More than just a martial arts club, we are a tight-knit community of students brought together by a shared passion for Aikido. Our nature is defined by the principles of respect, discipline, and continuous learning. Whether you're a beginner taking your first steps on the mat or an advanced practitioner honing your skills, the IIUM Aikido Club provides a welcoming space for growth and camaraderie.

Mission

to foster a culture of Aikido excellence, where individuals can discover their potential, build lifelong friendships, and contribute positively to the wider community. We believe in the transformative power of Aikido, not just as a martial art but as a way of life.

Fostering harmony, resilience, and lifelong learning through the art of Aikido, creating a community where individuals thrive both on and off the mat

Vision

"Joining IIUM Aikido Club was one of the best decisions I've made. The instructors are not only skilled but incredibly patient and supportive. The sense of community within the club is empowering, and I've gained not only Aikido skills but also lifelong friendships. It's a journey of self-discovery and personal growth. Highly recommended!"
- Syed Putra, President IIUM Aikido Club 23/24
